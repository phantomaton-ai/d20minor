## Monsters

What's an adventure without monsters? Here are a few creatures for the GM to use. The "Level" of a monster gives you a rough idea of how challenging it is. A party of four adventurers should be able to handle a monster of their own level.

### Giant Rat (Level 1)
*   **AC:** 12, **HP:** 7
*   **Abilities:** STR +0, DEX +2, CON +0, INT -2, WIS +0, CHA -2
*   **Attack:** Bite (+4 to hit, 1d4+2 damage)
*   **XP:** 25, **Treasure:** 1d4 GP

### Goblin (Level 1)
*   **AC:** 15, **HP:** 7
*   **Abilities:** STR -1, DEX +2, CON +0, INT +0, WIS -1, CHA -1
*   **Attack:** Shortsword (+4 to hit, 1d6+2 damage)
*   **XP:** 50, **Treasure:** 1d6 GP

### Skeleton (Level 1)
*   **AC:** 13, **HP:** 13
*   **Abilities:** STR +0, DEX +2, CON +2, INT -2, WIS -1, CHA -2
*   **Attack:** Shortsword (+4 to hit, 1d6+2 damage)
*   **XP:** 50, **Treasure:** None

### Wolf (Level 1)
*   **AC:** 13, **HP:** 11
*   **Abilities:** STR +1, DEX +2, CON +1, INT -2, WIS +1, CHA -1
*   **Attack:** Bite (+4 to hit, 2d4+2 damage)
*   **XP:** 50, **Treasure:** None

### Zombie (Level 1)
*   **AC:** 8, **HP:** 22
*   **Abilities:** STR +1, DEX -2, CON +3, INT -4, WIS -2, CHA -2
*   **Attack:** Slam (+3 to hit, 1d6+1 damage)
*   **XP:** 50, **Treasure:** None

### Orc (Level 2)
*   **AC:** 13, **HP:** 15
*   **Abilities:** STR +3, DEX +1, CON +3, INT -2, WIS +0, CHA -1
*   **Attack:** Greataxe (+5 to hit, 1d12+3 damage)
*   **XP:** 100, **Treasure:** 2d8 GP

### Owlbear (Level 3)
*   **AC:** 13, **HP:** 59
*   **Abilities:** STR +5, DEX +1, CON +3, INT -4, WIS +1, CHA -2
*   **Attack:** Beak (+7 to hit, 1d10+5 damage) and Claws (+7 to hit, 2d8+5 damage)
*   **XP:** 700, **Treasure:** 3d10 GP

### Bugbear (Level 3)
*   **AC:** 16, **HP:** 27
*   **Abilities:** STR +2, DEX +2, CON +1, INT -1, WIS +0, CHA -1
*   **Attack:** Morningstar (+4 to hit, 2d8+2 damage)
*   **XP:** 700, **Treasure:** 4d10 GP

### Ghoul (Level 4)
*   **AC:** 12, **HP:** 22
*   **Abilities:** STR +1, DEX +2, CON +0, INT -1, WIS +0, CHA -2
*   **Attack:** Bite (+2 to hit, 2d6+2 damage) and Claws (+4 to hit, 2d4+2 damage). A creature hit by a ghoul's attack must make a DC 10 Constitution ability check or be paralyzed for 1 minute.
*   **XP:** 1,100, **Treasure:** A tarnished silver ring worth 1d20 GP.

### Ogre (Level 4)
*   **AC:** 11, **HP:** 59
*   **Abilities:** STR +4, DEX -1, CON +2, INT -3, WIS -2, CHA -2
*   **Attack:** Greatclub (+6 to hit, 2d8+4 damage)
*   **XP:** 1,100, **Treasure:** 5d10 GP

### Troll (Level 5)
*   **AC:** 15, **HP:** 84
*   **Abilities:** STR +4, DEX +1, CON +5, INT -2, WIS -1, CHA -2
*   **Attack:** Bite (+7 to hit, 1d6+5 damage) and two Claw attacks (+7 to hit, 2d6+5 damage)
*   **Special:** The troll regains 10 hit points at the start of its turn. If the troll takes acid or fire damage, this trait doesn't function at the start of the troll's next turn. The troll dies only if it starts its turn with 0 hit points and doesn't regenerate.
*   **XP:** 1,800, **Treasure:** A small chest with 10d10 GP and a *Potion of Healing*.

### Vampire Spawn (Level 5)
*   **AC:** 15, **HP:** 82
*   **Abilities:** STR +3, DEX +3, CON +3, INT +1, WIS +1, CHA +2
*   **Attack:** Bite (+6 to hit, 1d6+3 damage plus 2d6 necrotic damage)
*   **Special:** The vampire regains 10 hit points at the start of its turn if it has at least 1 hit point and isn't in sunlight or running water.
*   **XP:** 1,800, **Treasure:** A fine cloak and a silver locket worth 3d20 GP.

### Wraith (Level 5)
*   **AC:** 13, **HP:** 67
*   **Abilities:** STR -2, DEX +3, CON +3, INT +1, WIS +2, CHA +2
*   **Attack:** Life Drain (+6 to hit, 4d8+3 necrotic damage). The target must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution ability check or its hit point maximum is reduced by an amount equal to the damage taken.
*   **XP:** 1,800, **Treasure:** None.

### Manticore (Level 6)
*   **AC:** 14, **HP:** 68
*   **Abilities:** STR +3, DEX +3, CON +3, INT -2, WIS +1, CHA -1
*   **Attack:** Bite (+5 to hit, 1d8+3 damage) and two Claw attacks (+5 to hit, 1d6+3 damage)
*   **Special:** Tail Spike. Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, range 100/200 ft., one target. Hit: 1d8+3 damage.
*   **XP:** 2,300, **Treasure:** A small, glittering gemstone worth 5d20 GP.

### Minotaur (Level 6)
*   **AC:** 14, **HP:** 76
*   **Abilities:** STR +4, DEX +0, CON +3, INT -2, WIS +3, CHA -1
*   **Attack:** Greataxe (+6 to hit, 2d12+4 damage)
*   **Special:** Charge. If the minotaur moves at least 10 feet straight toward a target and then hits it with a gore attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 2d8 damage.
*   **XP:** 2,300, **Treasure:** 6d10 GP

### Fire Giant (Level 9)
*   **AC:** 18, **HP:** 162
*   **Abilities:** STR +7, DEX -1, CON +5, INT +0, WIS +2, CHA +1
*   **Attack:** Greatsword (+11 to hit, 6d6+7 damage)
*   **XP:** 5,000, **Treasure:** A bag of 10d10 GP and a magical helmet.

### Young Dragon (Level 10)
*   **AC:** 18, **HP:** 133
*   **Abilities:** STR +4, DEX +0, CON +3, INT +2, WIS +0, CHA +2
*   **Attack:** Bite (+7 to hit, 2d10+4 damage)
*   **Special:** Fire Breath. The dragon breathes fire in a 15-foot cone. Everyone in the cone must make a DC 15 Dexterity ability check, taking 6d6 fire damage on a failed check, or half as much on a successful one. The dragon can't use its fire breath again for a minute.
*   **XP:** 5,900, **Treasure:** A hoard of 100d10 GP and a magic sword.

### Frost Giant (Level 10)
*   **AC:** 15, **HP:** 138
*   **Abilities:** STR +6, DEX -1, CON +5, INT -1, WIS +0, CHA +1
*   **Attack:** Greataxe (+9 to hit, 3d12+6 damage)
*   **XP:** 5,900, **Treasure:** 10d10 GP and a magical axe.

### Hydra (Level 10)
*   **AC:** 15, **HP:** 172
*   **Abilities:** STR +5, DEX +1, CON +5, INT -4, WIS +0, CHA -2
*   **Attack:** Bite (+8 to hit, 1d10+5 damage). The hydra has five heads. It can make one bite attack for each head.
*   **Special:** At the end of its turn, it grows two heads for each of its heads that died since its last turn, unless it has taken fire damage since its last turn.
*   **XP:** 5,900, **Treasure:** A pile of bones with 2d100 GP.

### Vampire (Level 15)
*   **AC:** 16, **HP:** 144
*   **Abilities:** STR +4, DEX +4, CON +4, INT +3, WIS +2, CHA +4
*   **Attack:** Bite (+9 to hit, 1d6+4 damage plus 3d6 necrotic damage)
*   **Special:** The vampire regains 20 hit points at the start of its turn if it has at least 1 hit point and isn't in sunlight or running water. If the vampire takes radiant damage or damage from holy water, this trait doesn't function at the start of the vampire's next turn.
*   **XP:** 13,000, **Treasure:** A velvet-lined coffin, a set of fine clothes, and a magical ring worth 100d10 GP.
